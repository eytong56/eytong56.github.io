Music Mania, National High School Game Academy 2020

July - August 2020

Created an original 2D platformer game using Unity in a team of five in two weeks as part of CMU's National High School Game Academy (NHSGA). As producer, led team meetings and delegated tasks. As lead 2D artist, created and implemented all character animations, start/end screens, and UI elements.

Music Mania is a 2D platformer game where you play as an aspiring conductor looking to collect instrument power-ups to defeat the instrument enemies and complete the orchestral performance.

Virasa, National High School Game Academy 2020

July 2020

Recreated the classic arcade game Galaga using Unity in a team of five in one week as part of CMU's National High School Game Academy (NHSGA). As one of the programmers, designed and implemented a player manager, enemy grid system, and programmed all enemy movements using Unity C#.
